T1

Ticket taxonomy: CC / Sales / Maintenance as three databases

Keep for now — decided 07-13

Three separate ticket databases, a relic of department-style organization. Assessed against two weeks of evidence (~247 triage audit rows + Verifier reports): the split produces essentially zero misrouting; the measured confusion was label-taxonomy drift, fixed 2026-07-13.

Cost of the split triplicated conventionsCost of merging now re-point every SOP + racetrack + Zap, twice

Decision banked: the three lifecycles are real; the three databases are not load-bearing. Target schema for the future owned store: one ticket entity, a Type field, three lifecycles, boards as filtered views. Don't pay the merge cost on Notion.
